Ministry Description # of Churches
FaithTech FaithTech is a growing movement of Christians interested in all things faith and technology. Our vision is to become a global digital hub for FaithTech conversations, integrations, and innovations. 78
Youth We empower the youth in our local churches to walk and grow in Christ's ways and teachings. 102
P2C - HQ Serving in our national and regional offices in Canada, Power to Change’s support staff provide administrative, marketing, design, accounting and support services for our ministries. They’re making Jesus known with their God-given skills and expertise. 172
P2C - Athletes in Action Sport is the one language that knows no boundaries and sees no distinctions. It unites cultures, transcends values and defies convention. That’s why Athletes in Action is committed to using the language of sport to communicate the most powerful message of all: God’s passionate love for His children. 155
P2C - Christian Embassy Christian Embassy inspires leaders with the person and values of Jesus. They are nonpartisan and work with ambassadors, members of parliament, and business and professional leaders in Canada. This work involves facilitating initiatives such as roundtables, leading ambassador tours to connect ambassadors with Canadian innovators and organizing networking lunches and international delegations. 398
P2C - Connecting Streams Connecting Streams connects the Church with the broken and marginalized. Volunteer-led teams present the gospel, promote discipleship relationships and help those in need to fully experience the Holy Spirit’s transformational power. 63
P2C - DRIME The heart of DRIME’s ministry can be seen through its teams on many street corners around the world. Using a combination of drama and one-on-one evangelism, DRIME shares the message of Jesus with anyone who will stop and watch. DRIME also nurtures the spiritual growth of its members, develops leaders, and empowers other outreach ministries through drama workshops and evangelism training. 37
P2C - Family Life The need to strengthen marriages and families is more pressing than ever. With the challenges of raising kids, complex family dynamics, busyness, financial stress and selfishness, staying together is often not easy. That’s why, for more than 25 years, FamilyLife Canada has been teaching, training and equipping families, giving them the tools to grow closer to each other and closer to God. 320
P2C - LeaderImpact What does it look like to live a life of true significance? Join a community dedicated to becoming better leaders and more effective professionals who are living lives with impact. LeaderImpact helps leaders explore their personal and professional lives through the lens of Christian faith, welcoming all who want to pursue a deeper purpose in life. 62
P2C - Students Students enter college or university with feelings of nervousness, excitement and anticipation. With newfound freedom, they find themselves suddenly exposed to a variety of opinions and people from all walks of life. Most students have never been given the opportunity to hear about Jesus’ love and the life-changing message of the gospel, and so they look for meaning in all the wrong places. Power to Change – Students helps students discover that Jesus is the answer to the deep longing in their hearts. 108
P2C - JFCPS For more than 35 years, the JESUS Film has been giving people around the globe the chance to hear the story of Jesus in their own language. The JESUS Film Church Planting Strategy is designed to encourage people to discover Jesus for themselves, equip church leaders, foster a community that is passionate about Christ and trained to follow-up and disciple new believers, and inspire spiritual multiplication. 418
P2C - Life Project The Life Project trains, encourages, and mobilizes a team of over 1,000 online mentors, and also helps churches across North America start their own mentoring ministry. 146
P2C - WHEN In the midst of busy schedules, women often wish they could find time to connect with other women at a heart level. Women’s Heart-Engaging Network (WHEN) is committed to providing opportunities in various languages for women to gather, share their stories and encourage each other. WHEN helps women discover how the grace of God can bring hope, strength and order to our lives and to those around us. 360

Pure... and simple.

Better Data, Better Decisions, Bigger Impact


Pure Impact is a cloud platform powering the modern church with networking, data analysis, and resource optimization. We call these Unity, Wisdom, and Stewardship. The net effect? A bigger impact footprint.

Heralding a Heavenly Kingdom of Data!

Unity
By unifying pastors, christian leaders and nonprofits in the K/W area, we will be uniquely positioned to make accessible currently non-public data. Pure Impact will also be building a suite of church apps that collect usage data, and contribute to a bigger, more complete picture the church in Kitchener / Waterloo.
Wisdom
With rich data in hand, Pure Impact will offer pastors and christian leaders vital analytics both at the church and regional, provincial, and national levels. This kind of visibility into key metrics surrounding the church today, empowers pastors to make targeted, data-driven decisions about their programs, sermons, venues, and further initiatives.
Stewardship
We are called to be the stewards of the resource God gave us. In addition to apps and analytics, Pure Impact will be a go-to shared resource exchange for churches to better offer what they have, and more quickly access what they don’t. A real-time view of the pulse of your church.

When you use our apps, you get more than cutting edge technology. You get insights into the inner workings of your church, in the areas that matter most. Attendance, giving, children & youth, demographics, events, small groups, programs and more come under a whole new light in Pure Impact dashboards. What is happening at the Church-level

Pure Impact has a simple mission to increase the impact footprint of today’s churches, powering the advancement of God’s kingdom with cloud technology. Our platform redefines what “Big Data” looks like in today’s church, making available population, income, demographic, religious, affiliation, marital, and education data at the regional, provincial, and national levels. Our mapping and geo-location technologies allows churches to make sense of this data in ways previously never thought possible.
